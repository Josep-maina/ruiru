About Ruiru Technical and Vocational College

Ruiru Technical and Vocational College is a TVET CDACC (TVET Curriculum Development, Assesment and Certification Council) acredited institution. We are located in Kiambu county, Ruiru Constituency, Gatongora ward, 2Km off Eastern Bypass (kamakis), 1Km off Karuguru Kwihota stage or 5km from Rainbow - St. Peter Catholic Church Route.
The college was established, under able mentorship of the Kabete National Polytechnic, with the aim of equipping youths with knowledge, skills and market driven competences for self-reliance: “Ujuzi wa kujitegemea.”
The college has a state of the art training equipment and currently accredited to offer CDACC Level 4-6 training in the following skill areas: Food production (Culinary Arts), Electrical Installation/ Engineering, Plumbing, Hairdressing, Beauty Therapy, ICT, Cybersecurity and Fashion Design. Ruiru TVC upholds CBET’s hands-on skills-acquisition approach.

The college also couples as an ICT hub in Ruiru Constituency, Gatongora Ward, with a modern 100 seater capacity Jitume ICT Lab for online job training and working by youths in the area.
The college also nurtures talents in creative arts (Drama & Music), Football, Handball, volleyball, badminton, athletics and indoor games.
The college has 3 intakes in a year: January, May & September.
