About Ruiru Technical and Vocational College

Ruiru Technical and Vocational College is a TVET CDACC (TVET Curriculum Development, Assessment and Certification Council) accredited institution. We are located in Kiambu county, Ruiru Constituency, Gatongora ward, 2Km off Eastern Bypass (kamakis), 1Km off Karuguru Kwihota stage or 5km from Rainbow - St. Peter Catholic Church Route.

The college has a state of the art training equipment and currently accredited to offer CDACC Level 4-6 training in the following skill areas: Food and Beverage Production (Culinary Arts), Electrical Installation/ Engineering, Plumbing, Hairdressing, Beauty Therapy, ICT, Cyber Security and Fashion Design. Ruiru TVC upholds CBET’s hands-on skills-acquisition approach.

The college also couples as an ICT hub in Ruiru Constituency, Gatongora Ward, with a modern 100 seater capacity Jitume ICT Lab for online jobs training for the community at large.
The college also nurtures talents in creative arts (Drama & Music), Football, Handball, volleyball, badminton, athletics and indoor games.
The college has 3 intakes in a year: January, May & September.

History of our Institution

The college was established, under able mentorship of the Kabete National Polytechnic, with the aim of equipping youths with knowledge, skills and market driven competencies for self-reliance: “Ujuzi wa kujitegemea.”
