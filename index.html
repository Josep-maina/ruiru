Ruiru Technical and Vocational College

Ruiru TVC is a Government sponsored TVET institution located in a serene environment conducive for learning in the industrial town of Ruiru, 2km off Eastern Bypass (Kamakis) along Karuguru Road.

WHY US?

  1. Quality Competency Based Education and Training (CBET)
  2. Professional career guidance to enable our students discover their potential passion, strength opportunities and skills required by the job market
  3. Vibrant student experience
  4. Our programs are demand driven, responsive and adaptive
  5. Timely completion and Competency
  6. Serene learning environment
